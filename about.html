About us

Located at 323 N Coast Hwy suite C. Oceanside, CA. We offer a wide array of fresh food – Roast Beef Sandwich, Tuna Melt, BLT Bagel, Chicken Salad, Peanut Butter Strawberry Banana, Fresh Seasonal Fruit Cup, Bagel with Egg, Ham, and Cheese, and Bagel with Cream Cheese. We use the freshest ingredients in preparing our food to provide the best quality and taste. Try our delicious food today!

Reviews

Abigail P.

If you are from New England/New York you will definitely love this spot. Prices are not the same as easy coast obviously but the atmosphere, and staff definitely make you feel at home. They have gluten free options but they do not prepare celiac friendly unless you ask. Cheese and egg on a plain bagel was $6 and their fancier bagel sandwiches are closer to $13. Really enjoyed the owners and the location is a bit tough to park if its busy but it's worth the walk. I'll be coming back to try their other stuff.

Elisa H.

10000/10 I want to come back here for breakfast and lunch every day I ordered the vegetarian - it's egg, cheese, onion, tomato, sprouts and avocado on your choice of bagel. I got Asiago cheese. SO GOOD. The service was quick and super friendly. I got half to go and they wrapped it up for me and put it in a bag. That's service! The inside isn't anything special, I like sitting outside but it's a small local place. If you want a bomb bagel come here!!

Kevin L.

One of the best bagels shops I've tried in California. The bagels are made fresh daily, and the service is excellent. Since the sandwiches are made to order- expect to wait 10-15 minutes, but it's worth it. They also have plenty of drinks, regular sandwiches, and sub sandwiches. The drip coffee is really good too. Highly recommend the pastrami egg and cheese. We also tried the chicken melt, and it's huge! We liked the bagels so much we usually order half or a full dozen to take home. The bagels keep pretty well for a couple of days.
